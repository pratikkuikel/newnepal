## Scope

Nepal has **753 local governments**:
- 6 **Mahanagarpalika** (Metropolitan Cities)
- 11 **Upamahanagarpalika** (Sub-Metropolitan Cities)
- 276 **Nagarpalika** (Municipalities)
- 460 **Gaunpalika** (Rural Municipalities)

## Responsibilities

- **Infrastructure Projects**: Roads, bridges, water supply, drainage
- **Local Permits & Licenses**: Business registration, construction permits
- **Waste Management**: Solid waste, sanitation services
- **Social Services**: Local health posts, community programs
- **Revenue Collection**: Property tax, business tax, local fees

## Key Entities

| Entity | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| `Municipality` | Local government unit with budget |
| `LocalProject` | Municipality-level development project |
| `Contractor` | Registered contractor executing projects |
| `LocalRevenue` | Tax and fee collection records |
| `ServiceDelivery` | Citizen services rendered |

## Transparency Features

- **Project Tracker**: Citizens can track progress of local projects
- **Expenditure Dashboard**: Real-time spending visualization
- **Contractor Performance**: Rating and history of contractors
